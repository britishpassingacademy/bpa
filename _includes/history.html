History

A little history to how we began!

  • Jan 2018

    BPA Is Founded!

    Tom Kharchi founds BPA to help develop the British game.

  • March 2018

    First Camps Are Announced

    Our first camps are announced and tickets go on sale

  • April 2018

    BPA announces Hydration partner:
    GATORADE!

    Gatorade become an official partner of BPA. They'll provide our camp participants with refreshments to keep them hydrated.

  • April 2018

    BPA announces Equipment sponsor:
    EP SPORTS!

    Following the Gatorade partnership, BPA announce their second partner, EP Sports. EP will be on hand to supply athletes with any equipment needs.

  • April 2018

    BPA announced Tech Partner:
    CLOUDRIGHT!

    BPA announce their official technology partner Cloudright. Cloudright are a tech consultancy focusing on cloud adoption for tech organisations.
